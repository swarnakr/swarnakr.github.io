Projects: Computer Vision

DST Project on Features for 3D applications

  • Features for point tracking applications such as Structure from Motion and 3D reconstruction, which perform better than the state-of-the-art on Object boundaries.
  • Traditional gradient-based methods fail at depth discontinuities due to object motion against a varying background. Our method works on the curve describing the object and thus recovers such features.
  • Extensive Tests were conducted in a Vehicle tracking scenario that is important in self-driving cars used in surveillance applications.

Submitted to IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2015
