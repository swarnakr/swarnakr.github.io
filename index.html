About Me

I am a CS PhD student at Duke University, working with Prof Carlo Tomasi. I'm interested in Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence, specifically in Machine Learning algorithms for Tracking.

Before this, I was a Project Officer at the Computer Vision Lab, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, where I also obtained a Master's degree working with Dr. Anurag Mittal. I did my undergrad at Anna University (MIT campus).
